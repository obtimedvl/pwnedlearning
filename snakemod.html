Google Snake Mod Guide

Go to google snake and bookmark it. After that go to the bookmark and press 'edit' and delete the url, instead paste the following code:

javascript: req = new XMLHttpRequest(); req.open('GET', 'https://raw.githubusercontent.com/DarkSnakeGang/GoogleSnakeCustomMenuStuff/main/custom.js'); req.onload = function() { eval(this.responseText + 'snake.more_menu();'); }; req.send();

after that press the bookmark and snake will get modded!
